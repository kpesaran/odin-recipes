Quiche

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Spinach
  • 1 Cup Ricotta Cheese
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 Tablespoons of flour
  • Few spoon of Mozzarella Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Pinch of salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix all ingrediants in bowl except for mozzerella cheese
  3. Put in oven for 40 minutes
  4. Put mozzarella cheese on top and bake for another 10 minutes
